Why Choose EVAcare Home Health

Welcome to EVAcare Home Health, the premier non-medical home healthcare agency dedicated to serving the residents of Lowell, Massachusetts, and surrounding communities. We understand that choosing the right care provider for yourself or your loved ones is an important decision, and we are here to assure you that EVAcare is the ideal choice.

One of the key factors that sets us apart is our commitment to professionalism and integrity. Let us be your partner in care, supporting you every step of the way on your journey to greater independence, well-being, and overall fulfillment. We provide:

Respite Care

Companionship Services

Medication Reminders

Meal Preparation and Nutrition

Personal Care Assistance

Light Housekeeping

Transportation services

Running errands and Shopping
