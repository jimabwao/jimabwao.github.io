About EVAcare Home Health

EVAcare Home Health is a non-medical home healthcare agency based at 355 Pawtucket Boulevard, Lowell, Massachusetts. EVAcare Home Health provides a dedicated team of caregivers ready to positively impact clients' lives, fostering independence, promoting well-being, and enriching the fabric of the local community. With a steadfast commitment to enhancing the lives of individuals in need, EVAcare agency prioritizes the lives of its clients to become a trusted provider of non-medical home healthcare services.

EVAcare Home Health operates with a deep sense of integrity and professionalism, ensuring that all caregivers undergo rigorous training and possess the necessary skills and expertise to deliver exceptional care. The agency adheres to the highest standards of safety, privacy, and ethical conduct, giving clients and their families peace of mind and reassurance throughout their care journey at the comfort of their home. For a non-medical home healthcare agency to run smoothly, we have equipped ourselves with a team of supporting characters to ensure its success; we have a team of professionals that have their motivations, goals, and unique personality traits which offer the best customer services to our clients.

Our Mission

At EVAcare Home Health, we have a set of goals and objectives that govern it to operate flawlessly and offer quality services to its clients. Below are some of the agency's missions to its success.

The agency ensures all clients' safety and well-being at their service

At EVAcare Home Health, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our clients. We implement appropriate safety measures, perform risk assessments, and train our caregivers to ensure a secure client environment, reducing the risk of accidents or injuries.

A mission to promote independence

The agency aims to help clients maintain their independence and autonomy by offering personalized care and support tailored to their needs. It may include promoting self-care skills and encouraging clients to remain engaged in daily activities and hobbies.

The caregivers can provide companionship and emotional support to the patients at their residents.

Loneliness and social isolation can significantly impact an individual's well-being. EVAcare Home Health will emphasize providing companionship and emotional support to their clients, fostering social interactions, and reducing feelings of isolation.

EVAcare Health Solutions collaborates with healthcare professionals.

EVAcare Home Health mission is to focus on non-medical aspects of care; we work collaboratively with healthcare professionals such as doctors, nurses, and therapists. This collaboration ensures a holistic care approach and facilitate communication and coordination among the care team.

EVAcare healthcare solutions focuses on the continuous improvement of the agency.

At EVAcare Home Health, we strive for continuous improvement to our services by monitoring client satisfaction, gathering feedback, and evaluate their practices to enhance the quality of care provided.

The agency ensures compliance and ethics.

EVAcare Home Health adheres to legal and ethical standards governing home care services. We maintain proper licensing, accreditation, and certifications and ensure our staff members are well-trained in compliance with regulations and ethical guidelines.
