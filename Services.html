Our Services

At EVAcare Home Health, we tailor our services to meet each client's unique requirements, providing personalized care plans to promote the client's well-being and independence at the comfort of their home.
EVAcare Home health offers the following services:
Respite Care

Offering temporary relief and support for family caregivers.

Assisting with caregiving responsibilities to allow caregivers time for self-care or other commitments.

Companionship Services

Engaging in conversation and providing emotional support.

Assisting with reading, writing, or engaging in hobbies.

Providing companionship and reducing feelings of isolation.

Accompanying clients on outings, appointments, or social activities.

Medication Reminders

Reminding clients to take their prescribed medications.

Assisting with medication organization and management.

Notifying healthcare professionals of any concerns or changes.

Meal Preparation and Nutrition

Planning and preparing nutritious meals according to dietary needs.

Assistance with feeding or ensuring proper hydration.

Monitoring dietary restrictions or allergies.

Personal Care Assistance

Help with bathing, grooming, and personal hygiene.

Assistance with dressing and getting in and out of bed.

Support with toileting and incontinence care.

Mobility assistance and transfers.

Light Housekeeping

Performing light cleaning tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, and sweeping.

Laundry and ironing services.

Changing bed linens and maintaining a clean and safe living environment.

Transportation services

Providing transportation to medical appointments, errands, or social outings.

Assisting with wheelchair accessibility or mobility aids.

Accompanying clients during transportation for support and safety.

Running errands and Shopping

Grocery shopping or picking up prescriptions.

Assisting with making purchases or managing personal finances.

Helping clients maintain independence and access necessary supplies.

Home Safety Assessments

Identifying potential hazards in the home and making recommendations for modifications or improvements.

Ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for clients.
